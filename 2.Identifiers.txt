Identifier rules
________________

It is a rule given to a variable or a function or a class.

1. Special characters are not allowed in the variables.
   Except _ underscore

2. First letter must be a character.

3. Numbers are not allowed to be a first character in Variables.

4. Number can be followed by after a character.

5. We can't exceed characters more than 72 for identifiers.


Eg:
____


Name() allowed

_name() allowed

Name$() not allowed

Name_value() allowed

1name() not allowed

name1()  allowed
